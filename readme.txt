This is a bullet hell mod loosely based on Azur Lane for Quake 2! In order to play the game, download this mod's files, compile it, and move the release dll to the game mod's folder. 

When playing this mod, you can play the game normally, except it will be a little bit harder. Each monster shoots in a bullet hell fashion! Howeverm to help you in your journey, there are loot drops that will help you beat your enemies. In addition, there are entirely new weapons that will aid you in your quest.
